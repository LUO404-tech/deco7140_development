Visceral Design

The focus of this section is ensuring the user has a positive first impression of the site. This includes layout choices, typography, and colour scheme that communicate clarity, accessibility, and brand identity. A strong first impression helps users feel confident and encourages them to continue exploring the platform.

Layout Gallery

These wireframes illustrate the main page layouts for desktop view, showing how a consistent structure guides users across different sections. Each page serves a distinct role: Homepage provides orientation and discovery, Community Hubs focus on interaction and sharing, Learning Studio offers structured resources, Community Support addresses help and rules, and News keeps users updated with the latest content.

Mobile Wireframes

To reflect a mobile-first design approach, here are the corresponding mobile layouts. Since many students primarily access content on their phones, these layouts ensure responsive design and maintain usability and visual consistency across devices.

Typography

Typography establishes a clear visual hierarchy and ensures readability across different screen sizes. Larger headings guide the eye, while smaller captions provide contextual detail without distraction.

Colour Scheme

The colour palette balances vibrancy with accessibility, ensuring strong contrast for readability and alignment with WCAG 2.1 guidelines. Each colour is carefully selected to reinforce brand identity while supporting usability.

Used for headings and navigation

Neutral: #f5f5f5
