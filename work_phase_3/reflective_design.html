Reflective Design

This section reflects on how our design aligns with values, ethical principles, and intended user experience. It covers Values Mapping, Imagery & Tone, Reflective Language, and Dynamic Content.

Values Mapping

These values extend from our WP2 Flip Cards and are mapped into actionable design features. They ensure our platform is inclusive, safe, and enjoyable.

♿ Inclusivity

Feature: Focus indicators, high contrast, labels

Rationale: Equal participation for all users.

🤝 Trust & Safety

Feature: Minimal forms (Email & Password)

Rationale: Protects privacy, builds confidence.

🌐 Belonging

Feature: Clear navigation & friendly language

Rationale: Helps users feel connected.

🎉 Fun & Creativity

Feature: Playful icons and emoji for hobbies

Rationale: Keeps site light-hearted and engaging.

Imagery & Tone

The imagery uses simple icons and emojis to represent community diversity and hobbies, avoiding heavy or distracting visuals. This ensures accessibility and keeps the tone friendly, inclusive, and playful.

🎮 🎭 📚 🎶

These icons reflect the four hobby categories from WP2 user research: Gaming, Cosplay, Anime & Collecting, and Music & Dance. They keep the design tone light-hearted, accessible, and aligned with our community’s identity.

Reflective Language

The wording across the site is chosen to reflect empathy, inclusivity, and trust. Instead of purely functional or technical commands, the language acknowledges the values that users care about: safety, fairness, and belonging.

“Welcome, let’s help you find your community.”
(emphasises inclusivity and belonging)
“Your information is private and secure.”
(reinforces trust and data protection)
“Everyone is encouraged to share their stories.”
(supports fairness, equity, and expression)

These examples show how reflective language strengthens the design values mapped earlier, while maintaining the friendly and inclusive tone reinforced by imagery and icons.
