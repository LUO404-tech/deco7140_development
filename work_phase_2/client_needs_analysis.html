WP2 - Client Needs Analysis

The Client Brief

About Us

We are a non-profit organisation dedicated to fostering social cohesion by empowering diverse communities to share their stories, connect with others, and access resources that enhance their visibility and influence.

This brief guides internal design teams at CCI to develop dynamic, sustainable and trustworthy websites that celebrate community identities across Australia.

Project Overview

Each site acts as a hub where members can share resources, highlight events, and build connections. No commercial activity (ads/sales); promotion of community-benefit events and services is acceptable. Sites must be responsive and meet accessibility best practice.

Community Focus

Chosen theme: Hobby & Subculture Communities — e.g., gaming, cosplay, crafts, niche interest groups.

Target Audience

Community Members seeking representation, resources, events, and belonging.

General Audience learning about the community’s culture and contributions.

Project Goals

Represent and celebrate community identity without stereotypes.

Provide clear, accessible information and resources.

Foster meaningful connections and mutual support.

Uphold ethical, inclusive, and culturally sensitive design.

Client Needs (Hobby/Subculture)

Client Goals

  • Promote social cohesion across diverse hobby/subculture groups.
  • Represent and support cultural diversity and niche identities.
  • Provide a sustainable, inclusive, modern digital platform.
  • Encourage community interaction and engagement.

User Needs

  • Find resources, services, and events relevant to their hobby.
  • Recognition and representation for diverse and marginalised groups.
  • Simple, friendly UI for browsing and participation.
  • Safe spaces to connect, share, and collaborate with peers.
