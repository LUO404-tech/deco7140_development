Relevant Ethical Issues for Our Project

  • Privacy & Data Protection: student-ID / real-name vs. safety and data minimisation.
  • Accessibility & Inclusivity: mobile-first, visual clarity, keyboard and screen-reader support.
  • Fairness & Equity: welcoming to beginners and international students; no exclusion or harassment.
  • Trust & Transparency: clear rules, no scams, accurate information and visible moderation.
